## How content gets surfaced

- The page route is `/blog/[slug]`, statically generated at build time.
- Frontmatter drives `<title>`, OpenGraph, canonical, and `Article` structured data.
- Headings (`h2`/`h3`) feed Google's "key moments" extraction and AI engines' citation choices, so keep them descriptive.

## AEO-friendly content tips

- Lead each section with a short, self-contained sentence — AI engines often cite the first sentence of a section verbatim.
- Use `<dl>` (term/definition) blocks for glossaries.
- Add a FAQ section with `FAQPage` schema on pages where it fits.
